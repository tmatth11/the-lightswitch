Meet The LightSwitch

The most anticipated "dumb-home" gadget of all time. Forget asking your home assistant to flip the switch, just do it yourself!

About

The LightSwitch is a revolutionary product that allows you to turn your lights on and off with the flick of a switch. No more asking your smart home assistant to do it for you. The LightSwitch is easy to install, durable, energy efficient, and affordable. It's the perfect solution for anyone who wants to simplify their life and take control of their lighting.
